Claude Skills
Built-in Superpowers for Claude.ai
These skills live inside Claude and activate with a simple command. No tool to open — just type them in any Claude conversation.
/intent
Intent Skill
Transforms a vague or rough marketing request into a structured, expertly-framed brief — then executes it. Before writing anything, Claude shows you exactly how it's reading your ask and waits for your sign-off. Keeps work on-track without back-and-forth.
When to use
When your request is rough or open-ended — messaging, positioning, campaign briefs, analyst comms, enablement content, and more.
/source-accuracy
Source Accuracy Protocol
Enforces citation discipline on any document Claude drafts from your materials. Every factual claim gets traced to a source. Unverifiable stats are flagged or removed — never invented. Delivers a clean draft plus a full source map.
When to use
Battlecards, analyst briefings, competitor profiles, external reports — any output where facts, figures, and quotes must trace to a real source.
