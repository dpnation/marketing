Add typical data split ratios — the most-searched practical follow-up.

After understanding what the three datasets are, practitioners immediately search "training validation test split ratio" or "70 20 10 split machine learning." This article answers "what is each" but never answers "how much of each." Add a section or callout with the common splits (70/15/15, 80/10/10, or the 60/20/20 for smaller datasets) with the factors that influence the ratio (dataset size, model complexity, use case). This fills the most common content gap in competing articles.
