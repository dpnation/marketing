Why AI cites them: High technical authority, schema markup, and the open-source community credibility that comes with being the platforms practitioners use daily. Their content is frequently updated as the evaluation landscape evolves.

Applause's counter: Applause's angle is fundamentally different — human-graded evaluation at scale, not automated metrics. Positioning the article explicitly as "the human-in-the-loop evaluation complement to automated LLM evaluation metrics" would differentiate it from the technical documentation space and capture queries from practitioners who've tried automated evaluation and found its limits.
